Lathe Setup Tips

Face first when possible so Z zero is clean and repeatable.
Keep stickout short. Long stickout changes chatter, taper, and tool life.
Confirm chuck pressure, jaw contact, and part stop before cycle start.

Touch-off Tips

Write down whether X was set in diameter or radius mode.
After touching Z face, retract in X or Z before jogging quickly.
Measure twice before changing an offset by a large amount.

Safe Approach / Retract

Rapid to clearance, feed near the part.
Single block first move after offset changes.
Retract clear before tool change, restart, or index.
